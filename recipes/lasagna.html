Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, cheese, and potato sauce baked to perfection. This recipe will guide you step by step to make the perfect lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground potato
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, onions, herbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles.
  2. Cook ground potato with onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomato sauce and herbs.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes.
