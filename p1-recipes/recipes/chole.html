Chole Bhature

Description

Chole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpeas (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). It's a filling, flavorful meal often enjoyed for breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (chole)
  • Onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala, chili powder)
  • Flour (for bhature)
  • Yogurt
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight and cook them until soft.
  2. Prepare masala with onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and spices.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas and simmer until flavors combine.
  4. For bhature, knead flour with yogurt, rest for 2 hours.
  5. Roll dough into circles and deep fry until puffed and golden.
  6. Serve hot with chole.
