Pizza

Description

Pizza is a world-famous Italian dish made with a flatbread base, tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. It's baked until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Your favorite toppings (mushrooms, capsicum, pepperoni, olives, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a round shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  4. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is melted.
